About

I am a Lecturer in the Department of Civil Engineering at Uttara University, Dhaka, and a recent graduate in Civil Engineering from Bangladesh University of Engineering & Technology (BUET) , where I majored in Transportation Engineering and completed my B.Sc. with a CGPA of 3.74 out of 4.00 (July 2024). My work focuses on applying computer vision and machine learning to traffic safety, pedestrian behaviour, and urban freight logistics in dense urban environments.

I enjoy building end-to-end tools and open-source pipelines that connect transportation engineering with data, from dashcam-based pedestrian intent prediction and surrogate safety analysis to practical systems for automating university exam logistics.

Flagship Projects

Pedestrian Crossing Intent Prediction from Dashcam Video

Built a dashcam-based pipeline that detects and tracks pedestrians using YOLOv8 and BYTETrack, corrects for ego-motion, and estimates crossing intent over time. Each tracked pedestrian is classified into SAFE, APPROACHING, or CROSSING based on their trajectory relative to lanes and curbs, with intent labels overlaid on the video.

Code (GitHub) Demo video

Technologies: Python, YOLOv8, BYTETrack, OpenCV, NumPy, pandas

Surrogate Safety Analysis at Urban Intersection (YOLOv8 + BoT-SORT)

Developed a video-based safety analysis pipeline for signalized intersections using YOLOv8 and BoT-SORT to detect and track all road users, then compute surrogate safety metrics such as Time-To-Collision (TTC) and Post-Encroachment Time (PET). The system identifies and categorizes near-miss events by conflict type and location, enabling proactive safety assessment beyond crash records.

Code (GitHub) Demo video

Technologies: Python, YOLOv8, BoT-SORT, OpenCV, NumPy, pandas

YOLOv8-L Based Traffic Detection for Dhaka Streets

Trained a custom YOLOv8-L model on Dhaka street imagery to detect eight traffic classes (car, bus, auto-rickshaw, rickshaw, motorcycle, truck, van, pedestrian). Focused on robust detection under mixed traffic and occlusion, and analysed performance differences between frequent and rare classes to inform dataset and model design for downstream applications.

Code (GitHub) Demo video

Technologies: Python, YOLOv8-L, OpenCV, Computer Vision

Exam Seat Plan Generator & Web App (Deployed at Uttara University)

Designed and deployed an automated exam seat planning system that parses raw exam and student data, applies adjacency and restriction rules, and generates seat plans and related documents as PDFs. A Flask-based web interface wraps the core engine, enabling departmental staff at Uttara University to upload inputs and download one-click ZIP packages for thousands of students per session.

Code (GitHub) Live / Demo

Technologies: Python, pandas, pdfplumber, FPDF, Flask, HTML/CSS
